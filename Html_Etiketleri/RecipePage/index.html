Spaghetti Bolognese

This classic Italian pasta dish is hearty, delicious, and easy to make. Perfect for family dinners!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 250g ground beef
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a pan and saut√© the chopped onion and garlic.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the diced tomatoes and let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti and enjoy!
